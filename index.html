üöÄ Quick Start Guide

Getting Started

  1. Create an Account: Click "Sign Up" and enter your name, email, and password (minimum 6 characters).
  2. Choose a Category: Select from 8 different quiz categories including General Knowledge, Science, History, Technology, and Coding languages.
  3. Select Difficulty: Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard based on your skill level.
  4. Take the Quiz: Answer 10 questions within 10 minutes. You can navigate between questions freely.
  5. View Results: See your score, review answers, and check your performance history.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

+

Click "Sign Up" on the login page, enter your full name, email address, and create a password (minimum 6 characters). Make sure to use your own personal email address.

Can I change my answers during the quiz?

+

Yes! You can navigate between questions freely and change your answers at any time before submitting the quiz.

What happens if I run out of time?

+

The quiz will automatically submit when the timer reaches zero. All answered questions will be scored.

How is my score calculated?

+

Your score is calculated as a percentage: (Correct Answers / Total Questions) √ó 100. Unanswered questions are counted as incorrect.

Can I review my answers after submitting?

+

Yes! After completing a quiz, click "Review Answers" to see all questions, your answers, and the correct answers.

How many questions are in each quiz?

+

Each quiz contains 10 questions. There are 240 total questions across all categories and difficulty levels.

Is my data saved?

+

Yes, your account information and quiz history are saved in your browser's localStorage. Your data persists between sessions.

Can I take the same quiz multiple times?

+

Absolutely! You can retake any quiz as many times as you want. Each attempt is saved in your score history.

üí° Tips & Tricks

  • Read Carefully: Take your time to read each question and all options before answering.
  • Use Review Feature: Mark difficult questions for review and come back to them later.
  • Watch the Timer: Keep an eye on the timer - it changes color to warn you when time is running low.
  • Navigate Freely: Use the question indicators at the bottom to jump to any question.
  • Check Your Answers: Always review your answers before submitting to catch any mistakes.
  • Practice Regularly: Take quizzes from different categories and difficulty levels to improve your knowledge.

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

Can't log in?

Make sure you're using the correct email and password. If you forgot your password, you'll need to create a new account with a different email.

Quiz not loading?

Refresh the page. Make sure all files (index.html, script.js, quiz-data.js, styles.css) are in the same folder.

Timer not working?

Check your browser console for errors. Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings.

Score history not showing?

Your history is stored in browser localStorage. If you clear browser data, your history will be lost. Make sure you're logged in.

üìß Need More Help?

If you're experiencing issues or have questions not covered here:

  • Check the browser console for error messages
  • Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled
  • Try clearing your browser cache and reloading

Note: This is a client-side application. All data is stored locally in your browser.
